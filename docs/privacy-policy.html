Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

Final Round ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application on iOS or Android.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Profile information (target role, skills, experience level)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Location (optional, for job recommendations)

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the app:

  • Interview practice sessions and responses
  • Performance analytics and scores
  • App interaction data

Audio and Camera Data

With your permission, we may access:

  • Microphone: To record your interview practice responses for AI-powered transcription and feedback
  • Camera: For live camera preview during interview practice sessions and eye contact tracking

Audio recordings are processed to provide feedback and are stored securely. You can delete your recordings at any time.

Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Show relevant job opportunities in your area
  • Display salaries in your local currency

Platform-Specific Features

The following sections describe how certain features work differently on iOS and Android devices.

üçé iOS-Specific: Face Data and TrueDepth Camera (Eye Contact Tracking)

On iOS devices, our app uses Apple's ARKit framework and the TrueDepth camera (on supported devices) to track your eye contact. This feature helps you improve your non-verbal communication skills.

What We Collect:

  • Face orientation and gaze direction to determine if you are looking at the camera
  • Eye contact percentage calculated from gaze tracking data

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Facial geometry, face mesh, or 3D face maps
  • Biometric data or facial recognition identifiers
  • Facial expressions or emotion data
  • Face images, photos, or face prints

How Face Data is Processed:

  • On-device processing only: All face tracking is performed locally on your device using Apple's ARKit framework. Raw face tracking data never leaves your device.
  • Real-time processing: Gaze direction data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after calculating the eye contact percentage.
  • No biometric identification: We do not use face data to identify you or any other person.

Storage and Sharing:

  • Stored: Only the calculated eye contact percentage (a simple number) is stored as part of your session analytics.
  • Not shared: Face data and eye contact metrics are never shared with third parties.
  • Deletion: Eye contact metrics are deleted when you delete your interview session or account.

You can disable camera access at any time in your device settings, which will disable the eye contact tracking feature.

ü§ñ Android-Specific: Face Detection and Eye Contact Tracking

On Android devices, our app uses Google ML Kit Face Detection to track your eye contact during interview practice sessions. This feature helps you improve your non-verbal communication skills.

What We Collect:

  • Face detection data to determine if your face is visible and oriented toward the camera
  • Eye open probability values to estimate eye contact
  • Eye contact percentage calculated from face orientation data

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Facial geometry, face mesh, or 3D face maps
  • Biometric data or facial recognition identifiers
  • Facial expressions or emotion data beyond basic eye tracking
  • Face images, photos, or face prints

How Face Data is Processed:

  • On-device processing only: All face detection is performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. Raw face tracking data never leaves your device.
  • Real-time processing: Camera frames are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after calculating the eye contact percentage. No images are stored.
  • No biometric identification: We do not use face data to identify you or any other person.
  • CameraX integration: We use Android's CameraX library to access the camera securely and efficiently.

Storage and Sharing:

  • Stored: Only the calculated eye contact percentage (a simple number) is stored as part of your session analytics.
  • Not shared: Face data and eye contact metrics are never shared with third parties.
  • Deletion: Eye contact metrics are deleted when you delete your interview session or account.

Android Permissions:

  • CAMERA: Required for the live camera preview and eye contact tracking feature.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Required to record your interview responses for AI analysis.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Optional, used for local job recommendations and currency detection.
  • INTERNET: Required to sync your data and access AI services.

You can disable camera or microphone access at any time in your device settings (Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Final Round ‚Üí Permissions), which will disable the corresponding features.
