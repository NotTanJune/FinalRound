Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Why does the app need microphone access?

The microphone is used to record your interview practice responses so our AI can transcribe and analyze them, providing personalized feedback to help you improve.

Why does the app need camera access?

The camera provides a live preview during practice sessions and enables eye contact tracking. On iOS, we use ARKit; on Android, we use Google ML Kit‚Äîboth process face data entirely on your device.

Why does the app need location access?

Location helps us show you relevant job opportunities in your area and display salaries in your local currency. This is completely optional.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage (Supabase) to protect your data on both iOS and Android. Your interview recordings are private and can be deleted at any time.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to receive a verification code via email.
